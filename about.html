Our Mission

Founded in 2015, Interior Design Organization (IDO) is a professional development group for students pursuing a degree in Interior Architecture at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. IDO fosters a supportive community of aspiring interior designers, offering opportunities for peer connection, skill-building, and professional networking to help students thrive throughout their college experience and prepare for their future careers

We are...

  • A student-run organization
  • A resource for SoHE’s design focused majors
  • Open to anyone interested in interior design!

We run two to four total events each month between our two categories:

Networking Events

  • Alumni Q&As
  • Professional Panelists
  • Firm Tours
  • IIDA Events

Socials

  • Trivia Nights
  • Study Sessions
  • Software Workshops
  • Leopolds/game night
