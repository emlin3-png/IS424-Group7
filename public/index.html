Our Mission

Founded in 2015, Interior Design Organization (IDO) is a professional development group for students who are passionate about design, whether they are pursuing degrees in Interior Architecture, Design, Innovation & Society, Landscape Architecture, and more. IDO fosters a supportive community of aspiring interior designers, offering opportunities for peer connection, skill-building, and professional networking to help students thrive throughout their college experience and prepare for their future careers.

Who we are

  • We are a student-run organization, serving as a resource for the School of Human Ecology's design-focused majors We are open to anyone interested in interior design!

We run 2-4 events each month:

Networking Events

  • Alumni Q&As
  • Professional Panelists
  • Firm Tours
  • IIDA Events

Socials

  • Trivia Nights
  • Study Sessions
  • Software Workshops
  • Leopolds/game night
